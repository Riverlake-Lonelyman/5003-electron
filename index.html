Precautions

This study aims to analyze and understand the emotional changes of viewers while watching videos, in order to better comprehend the impact of video content on emotions.

  • Once you agree to participate in this study, we will obtain your camera usage permission to capture your facial expressions while you watch the videos. We promise that all data collected will be strictly confidential, used solely for research purposes, and will not be used for any commercial purposes or shared with third parties.
  • You have the right to withdraw from this study at any time without any adverse effects. If you choose to withdraw, we will immediately cease collecting your data.
